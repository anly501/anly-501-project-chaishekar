Introduction

As a societal and economic challenge, crime has a negative impact on people's daily lives and the health of the economy. Every society and community has its own unique norms and customs when it comes to criminal behavior. Education, income inequality, and employment status are all variables that have been found to influence crime rates in previous studies of crime prediction. Methods for analyzing and preventing criminal behavior are the focus of crime analysis and prevention. Our system can visualize high-crime-risk areas and predict which areas are most likely to experience criminal activity. Crime data analysts can assist law enforcement officials in spurring the investigation of crimes as more and more systems are becoming computerized. Rather than concentrating on tracking down specific criminals, the focus is on the criminality of locations. Crime location and prediction will be into machine learning models.
